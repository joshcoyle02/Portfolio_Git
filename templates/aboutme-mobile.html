Who am I?

I'm a recent graduate from the University of the West of Scotland where I achieved a first class honors degree in Computing Science. Throughout my time at university, I discovered my passion for engineering solutions through problem solving and digital application. Although my early years of university sparked my pursuit of learning software development fundamentals and computer architecture, It wasn't until my final year where I took a class in Machine Learning where the fire was lit fully. This brought me to applying machine learning to my dissertation where designed a model to predict UFC matches and give fighter statistic analysis which was graded an A. My UFC predictor model is on my portfolio if you would like to check it out.
